About

Our software development team works day in and day out to provide users the most current crime related data in Prince George’s County. The following map software was developed utilzing the Prince George’s Crime Incident Dataset 2017 and Public Safety API to generate map data. Moving forward we will continue to provide up to date information and more features will soon follow as more data and technology becomes available.
